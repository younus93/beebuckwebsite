About Us.

Thai Beebuck Company Limited is Thailand’s local directory search engine that provides local search related service to users across Thailand through multiple platforms such as websites, mobile websites, Apps ( Android & IOS ). Beebuck has introduced the “Beehive” service to create unlimited Pages, Groups, and Sub-Groups. Each level has mounted with a directory feature in which you can access the information of your team, batch, group, etc. The aim of this service to help people stay connected and never lose contact for life. Beebuck has initiated a “Cards” service for all the users. This service aim at business cards and membership cards carrying task to make it more convenient. This feature helps people to digitalize their existing physical cards and save them in the application. Making it more conveniently actionable and accessible to users through our app anywhere and anytime.

Sign Up & Sign In

It is easy to sign up in Beebuck. All you need to provide is your name, email id, and password of your own. It's also easy to set up Beebuck for Institutions /Organizations/ Companies as well as smaller groups. The structure can easily be personalized as per the specific needs of your Institution. Once the profile for the Institution is created you can start inviting people to join or people can send requests to join. Admins will be accepting the requests. Under one Institution profile, any number of groups can be created and Under one group any number of subgroups can be created.

Profile

The moment you signed in, you can build your profile by providing your contact details and other information such as where you work, what work you doing, about your education, your past work experience, you can also write about yourself, you can add you social accounts and Tags.

Friends List

Whenever you view your friend’s list in others you usually see their name and the mutual friends between you two. Unlike other applications, Beebuck provides two views on the friend’s list. 1. Card view 2. List view. In the card view, your friend’s profile will be shown as a business card that provides your friend’s profile picture, full name, mutual friends, nickname, date of birth, phone number, and email.

Directory List

On this page you can quickly get an overview of all the different directories you are a member of and how many users are there in each directory. The directory list also provides you with a card view and list view. Card view helps you to contact them with one touch. You can be a member of a directory as many as you want.

Events Calender

You can view all your schedules here. You can add events by toggling to the particular date you want or by clicking the plus icon. Events are of two types, public event, and private event. You can create a private event and send invites to the users to join. Public events are open to all users in the Beebuck, users can search the event and join the event without an invite.

Notifications

View all notifications in one place. Friend requests, Birthday alerts, Invites to join events, invites from groups, see people who liked your posts, commented and shared your posts. You can also see who posted content on your timeline.

BeeHive View

Beebuck fulfills for any groups with any number of peeps. Chances are more that you will be a member of more than one institution, college, association, group, school, club, and more. On this hive page you can quickly get an overview of all the different pages you own, groups and subgroups you are a member of, and how many users are there in each page, group, and subgroups

Admin tools

These tools will help to create groups and subgroups, handles member requests, can create directories, invite members, check membership counts, edit the information details, leave and delete.

Settings

Never forget to update your profile whenever needed. Tap your profile picture to upload your display picture. If you change your phone number or move to a new address, your friends won’t lose your contact. Just update the information here so it will be updated on your friends’ phones automatically.
